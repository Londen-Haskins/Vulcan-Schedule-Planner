Welcome to the Vulcan Schedule Planner!

Home

This will take you here, to the Home page, where you can make your way to the other pages here in our website! You can also find our motivation for creating this project, and link to the the university's website below. We hope this helps you with your time at Cal U!

Scheduler

The Scheduler page will allow you to create a new schedule from scratch or edit an existing one if you're a returning user (and have your document)!
The classes are listed below by a few categories, so make sure you read the section headers. The classes you've passed can be checked off, and after pressing the "Update Schedule" button at the bottom, your schedule for the next semester will be generated.
Click the "Save Schedule" to save your schedule file to your computer.

Project Page

This will take you to the webpage we used to track and update our progress on the project. For those interested in our weekly progress are welcome to visit the site, but that is the only purpose is serves.

Class Search

The Class Search page will allow you look up various classes from this site's database, providing the major, name, and credit count for the specific classes.
